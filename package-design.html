Package Design

“ Packaging re-design for Farmer’s Market cookies, with a look and feel just like homemade ”

About the Project:

This project was a package re-design for the Farmer’s Market brand of cookies. The goal was to create a better-looking design as well as use materials better for the environment. The original packaging was a plastic clamshell with little design on it. This redesign implements colours and fonts to create a welcoming and cohesive design to match the slogan saying "just like homemade". In addition to the design a die line also had to be created for the outer and inner boxes, as well as the stickers to keep the box shut.
